Retail

We have successfully pioneered the rural retail model in India and operate a network of around 1000+ outlets across India. We serve over 3 million farmers by offering a range of agri-input products, services and farm advisory solutions.

Coromandel forayed into the Retail business in 2007 to establish closer consumer relations and enrich farmer lives by offering end to end quality products at the right time and providing marketing solutions within easy reach under a single roof. Today, we have successfully established a network of 1000+ outlets across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka and Tamil Nadu while also foraying into Maharashtra. Retail is a multi-brand outlet for various kinds of products and services which provide complete solutions from Seed to Harvest. Farmers can avail market leading solutions from top brands in the agri-input industry in our stores

What differentiates us in the market are our services, which add an intangible value to farming. Advisory services along with Soil Testing are part of our intrinsic services.
