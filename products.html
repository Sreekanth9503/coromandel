Overview

As India’s leading agri-solutions provider, we deliver products and services across the crop life cycle, from seed to harvest. By promoting technology & mechanization, we enhance resource efficiency & farm profitability.

Fertilisers and Nutrients

Fertilizer

We are the largest manufacturer & marketer of complex fertilisers in the country. Our wide range of products is customized to meet the unique needs of crops and soils. Through innovation & tailored solution. Our efforts help farmers boost productivity & achieve higher profitability.

Speciality Nutrients

To address the issues concerning the imbalanced crop nutrient practices, declining fertiliser efficiency and low factor productivity, Coromandel forayed into Speciality Nutrients segment about two decades back.

Organic

Nearly 70% of the Indian arable soil is organic carbon deficient. With a vision to enrich soil health, we are promoting organic fertilisers derived from natural sources like city waste (organic only), sugarcane molasses & by-products, oil cakes & gypsum.

Crop Production

The Crop Protection business produces insecticides, fungicides, herbicides and plant growth regulators and markets these products in India and abroad. Coromandel is the second largest manufacturer of Malathion and is the major manufacturer of Phenthoate in Asia. By taking over M/s Sabero Organics in 2011, Coromandel has added many technicals to its range of products and has climbed to a coveted position among top 5 companies in Indian Pesticide Industry. The company has global registrations in 62 countries and has strong presence in export markets of Latin America, SE Asia & Africa.

Bio Products

The Bio Products Division provides safe, sustainable, and eco-friendly agricultural solutions that align with global efforts to reduce chemical residues in the environment and on crops. We are proud to hold a global leadership position in the Azadirachtin market, with a strong footprint in more than 40 countries across the Americas, Europe, and Asia. By manufacturing and promoting green, natural, organic, and sustainable agri-inputs—including Azadirachtin-based crop protection solutions, a patented and distinctive range of Plant Growth Promoters (PGPs), as well as microbial bio-fertilizers and biopesticides—the division is committed to supporting bountiful harvests for a healthier planet. Recognizing the rising demand for natural pest control and plant care solutions among home and garden enthusiasts, we have launched the “Gromor Natural” brand.

Retail

We have successfully pioneered the rural retail model in India and operate a network of around 1000+ outlets across India. We serve over 3 million farmers by offering a range of agri-input products, services and farm advisory solutions. The retail centers have three key offerings – Soil Testing, Crop Diagnostics and Farm Mechanization services. We also cater to the veterinary segment.
